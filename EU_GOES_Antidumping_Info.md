## Duty Structure

### Minimum Import Price (MIP) System

‚ö†Ô∏è **Important:** This measure uses a **Minimum Import Price (MIP)** system instead of traditional ad valorem dumping duties.

Under the MIP system:
- Duties are applied when imports enter the EU below specified minimum prices
- Three different MIPs apply to three product categories based on core loss and thickness
- Individual company dumping margins are **not** specified
- The system provides flexibility while protecting EU industry

**Key Features:**
1. **Variable Duties:** Calculated based on the difference between the import price and the MIP
2. **Product Categories:** Three categories with different MIPs
3. **Certification Required:** Importers must provide mill certificates and commercial invoices
